Bio.

I am a 26-year-old Latin American with an MBA and ten years of practical web development experience. I started building websites when I was just 16 years old after learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript online. Since then, I have built websites for: a small real estate firm, a few non-profits, Hachette Book Group, Rutgers University, and Tisha Creative.

The World Wide Web has changed significantly in the past ten years, so I have tried to stay current by continuously reading: A List Apart, CSS Tricks, Smashing Magazine, Tuts+, W2C Schools, and more. In addition, I have adopted best practices by learning from and using several frameworks such as: HTML5 Boilerplate, jQuery, Twitter Bootstrap, WordPress, and Zurb Foundation.

In addition to my web development abilities, I also have management experience. For the past two years I have directly supervised five interns and indirectly supervised a staff of 30+ students in my role as Assistant Director of Marketing at Rutgers University. Prior to that, I directly supervised a student intern in my role as Web Developer for the School of Arts and Sciences, and prior to that, I was a student manager. As a Student Affairs professional, I have also mentored students, provided support, reviewed resumes, and provided career advice based on my personal experiences.
