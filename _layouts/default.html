Bio.

I am a 27-year-old Latin American with an MBA and ten years of practical web development experience. I started building websites when I was just 16 years old after learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript online. Since then, I have built websites for: a small real estate firm, a few non-profits, Hachette Book Group, Rutgers University, Tisha Creative, and the National Basketball Association.

The World Wide Web has changed significantly in the past few years, so I have stayed current by continuously reading: A List Apart, CSS Tricks, Smashing Magazine, Tuts+, W2C Schools, and more. In addition, I have adopted best practices by learning from and using several frameworks such as: HTML5 Boilerplate, jQuery, Twitter Bootstrap, WordPress, and Zurb Foundation.
